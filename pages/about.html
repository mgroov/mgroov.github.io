About Us

At Pet Rock Camp, we understand the bond that forms between an owner and their pet rock. Each rock, whether it's a shiny little pebble or polished limestone, possesses its own individuality and story. Located in the heart of Las Cruces, NM, Pet Rock Camp provides a fun and safe environment for your cherished companions. Our team is composed of compassionate individuals who ensure every rock feels valued and loved. Pet Rock Camp goes above and beyond to create memorable experiences for your pet rock. From Sunbathing Sessions under the warm southern New Mexico sun to Storytelling Time where rocks can hear legendary myths about famous rocks from around the world, your pet rock is in for an adventure they won't forget. Whether you are on vacation or simply need trusted supervision during the work week, rest assured that your pet rock will enjoy a safe and entertaining stay at Pet Rock Camp. Welcome to our Pet Rock Daycare and boarding facilities; we are thrilled to have you and your precious stone join us!
