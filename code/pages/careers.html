Mineral Specialist

Job Description:
As the Chef for Rocktopia Pet Rock Summer Camp, you will be an integral part of creating a memorable experience for campers through delicious and nourishing minerals. Your culinary skills will contribute to the overall success of the camp by ensuring that campers are fueled for a summer of excitement and exploration. Responsibilities:
Menu Creation: Develop a creative and balanced menu that caters to campers' dietary needs.
Meal Preparation: Prepare breakfast, lunch, and dinner for campers, incorporating fresh, seasonal minerals and ensuring high-quality and flavorful meals.
Pet Rock Nutrition: Design and prepare specialized meals for pet rocks, taking into account their unique nutritional requirements.
Food Safety: Maintain the highest standards of food safety and hygiene in all aspects of meal preparation, storage, and service.
Camp Engagement: Interact with campers and engage them in culinary activities, teaching them basic cooking skills and encouraging their interest in food.
Collaboration: Work closely with the camp's activities team to coordinate meal times and any special events or themed meals.
Flexibility: Adapt menus based on dietary restrictions and allergies, ensuring all campers can enjoy their meals.

Senior Gravity Counselor

Job Description:
As the Gravity Counselor at Rocktopia Summer Camp for Rocks, you will guide and facilitate gravity-related activities, adventures, and learning experiences for our rock campers. Your creativity, enthusiasm, and understanding of geology will help rocks of all backgrounds experience the joy and wonder of defying gravity.
Responsibilities:
Gravity Workshops: Plan and lead engaging workshops that teach campers about gravity's role in the rock world, including the forces that shape landscapes.
Rock Adventures: Organize exciting excursions where rocks can experience gravity in action, such as guided hikes and geological formations.
Individualized Guidance: Provide one-on-one guidance for rocks looking to develop their relationship with gravity and enhance their self-confidence.
Campfire Stories: Share captivating stories about gravity's influence on rocks, from the formation of mountains to the erosion of riverbeds.
Gravity Games: Develop and oversee interactive gravity-themed games and challenges that encourage teamwork and problem-solving among rocks.
Safety: Ensure the safety of all campers by adhering to proper safety protocols during all gravity-related activities.
Collaboration: Work closely with fellow counselors to integrate gravity-themed content into broader camp activities.
