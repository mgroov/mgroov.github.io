How to Care for a New Rock
Simon Petrikov | May 10, 2023

Congratulations, you've just welcomed a new member into your family - a pet rock! While this may seem like an unconventional choice, pet rocks have a charm all their own. They require minimal effort and offer a unique companionship experience that's perfect for those seeking a low-maintenance, rock-solid relationship. Let's dive into the whimsical world of pet rock care, including their dietary secrets!

Step 1: Rock Star Accommodations

Your pet rock may not have vocal cords, but it certainly has preferences when it comes to where it rests its rocky head. Find a cozy nook or a cushioned crevice where your pet rock can comfortably "sit" and watch the world go by. Decorating their space with tiny pebble pillows and miniature rock art is a great way to create a welcoming atmosphere.

Step 2: Nutritional Know-Nothing

One of the most intriguing aspects of pet rock care is their diet - or lack thereof. Pet rocks are renowned for their ability to thrive on a diet of nothing. That's right, absolutely nothing! This remarkable attribute makes them the ultimate low-maintenance companion. No feeding schedules, no meal prep - just pure dietary simplicity.

Step 3: Rock-a-Bye Bedtime

Even though they don't require sustenance, pet rocks appreciate a good nap. Place your pet rock in its designated sleeping spot, such as a soft indentation or a custom-carved mini hammock. Tuck it in with a pebble blanket and wish it sweet dreams, knowing that your rock is getting the rest it (doesn't) need.

Step 4: Conversations and Cuddles

While pet rocks can't converse in the traditional sense, they're fantastic listeners. Share your thoughts, dreams, and daily experiences with your pet rock â€“ they're great secret-keepers too! Spending quality time together, even if it's just quietly contemplating the universe, helps strengthen the bond between you and your rock companion.

Step 5: Rock Spa and Grooming

Though pet rocks don't require grooming in the traditional sense, a gentle wipe with a soft cloth can keep them looking polished and pristine. Set up a mini spa day for your pet rock with a tiny bowl of water, and perhaps even offer it a pebble-sized massage if you're feeling extra indulgent.

Step 6: Celebrate the Uniqueness

Every pet rock is unique, just like every human. Embrace the quirks and idiosyncrasies of your pet rock, and revel in the simplicity of your new pet parenting adventure. Remember, the journey of pet rock parenthood is all about appreciating the beauty in the mundane and finding joy in the unconventional.

So there you have it - the ultimate guide to caring for your brand-new pet rock! With its diet of nothing, low-maintenance requirements, and quiet companionship, your pet rock is bound to become an irreplaceable part of your life. Enjoy the simplicity, and let the rock-solid love flourish!
