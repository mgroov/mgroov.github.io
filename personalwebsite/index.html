Hello! I am Matthew Groover I am a Graduate Student at NMSU (New Mexico State University). My main focus is in game development and design. I graduated from NMSU with a bachelors in computer science in Spring of 2022. My hobbies include Game Development Club, Reading and Gaming.

I have gained proffesional experience in software development through an Internship over the Summer of 2020. Through this Internship I gained respect for the challenges that face proffesional development and prototyping.

My current goals is to finish my Masters Degree and project. Then go out into the video game development industry. I believe that by completing this project I will gain appropriate understanding of the challenges faced in the video game development cycle.
