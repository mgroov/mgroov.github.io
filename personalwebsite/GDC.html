NMSU GDC

NMSU GDC is a student run school organization focused on teaching and engaging in pratical game development. I have been active in this club since my freshman year.

Throughout my time period in this club we have group developed multiple games. This ranged from Virtual Reality titles to 2d rpg games. Through this experience I have learned both how to lead and be apart of a game development team. Furthermore the GDC actively engages in community services such as a charity stream each november.
